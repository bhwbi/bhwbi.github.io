Chapter 1 - Book Keeping

Concept and meaning of Book keeping

Book Kepping is art of recording business dealing in a set of books.

Book keeping is that branch of knowledge which tells us how to keep a record of economic activities or financial transaction of a concern.

Objectives of book keeping

  • To identify the financial transaction , out of large number of event.
  • To measure identified financial transaction in monetary term say Rs....or $...etc
  • To classify the the account involve on financial transaction into personal,real and nominal account
  • To have permanent record of each transaction by applying rules.
  • To help for ascertaining the combined effect of all the transaction during an accounting period on the financial position.

Meaning and concept of Accounting

Accounting is the means of measuring and reporting the result of economics activities .

It is also defined as the identifying, measuring,and recording and communicating of financial information.

Function of Accounting

  • Record keeping and information presenting function

    • The primary function of accounting relates to recording , classifying,and summarizing financial transaction i.e.journal,ledger and preparation of financial statements that facilitate to know operating result and financial position.

  • Managerial function

    • Managerial level is directly concerned with planning and decision making programme which may mislead in the absence of accounting.

  • Legal requirement function

    • Auditing is compulsory in case of registered company which is impossible without accounting.

  • Reporting function

    • Accounting is the language of business that communicates the result of the concern in the form of report to the stakeholders.

Objectives of Accounting

  • To maintain proper record of business transaction

    • The main objective of accounting is to keep record of financial transaction into approriate book of account in a systematic and complete manner for future reference.

  • To determine operating result

    • One of the main objective of accounting is to calculate the profit or loss of concern during a particular period.

  • To disclose the financial position

    • Detemining profit or loss is not enough . The businessman must know about his financial position i.e. where he stand,what he owes and owns?

  • To help in decision making

    • The other prime objective is to review plan and decide business policies in the light of past record after carefull analysis and interpretation.

  • To assess the tax amount

    • A businessman compulsory contibutes some amount of profit to the government for performing business activities which is known as tax.Therefore,the accurate and the systematic record of business financial transaction facilitate to estimate and determine the exact amount of tax.

Advantage or importance of Accounting

  • Facilitate to replace memory

    • Business transaction are innumerable ,varied and complex as a result one can't recall entire transaction whenever these are required.

  • Documentary evidence

    • Accounting record can also be used as evidence in the court to file case and settle the dispute.

  • Facilitate to calculate net profit or loss

    • It facilitate to calculate net profit or loss of a concern with the help of complete records of transactions by preparing income statement.

  • Facilitate to disclose the financial position

    • Financial position i.e. assets, liabilities and capital can be ascertained by preparing balance sheet at the end of a particular period.

  • Facilitate to prevend frauds and errors

    • The proper accounting system facilities to detect and prevent frauds ,cheating,and embezzlement by auditing the book of account internally and externally.

  • Helpful to management and others

    • Accounting records help to management for evaluting performance,planning and decisions making .

Scope or utility of Accounting

  • Accounting for business concern

    • Accounting is used to determine operating result i.e. profit or loss during a particular period and show financial position at the end of a particular period by preparing icome statement and balance sheet.

  • Accounting for non-profit making concern

    • These organization are existed in a society to provide service to the general public.

  • Accounting for government and non-government organization
